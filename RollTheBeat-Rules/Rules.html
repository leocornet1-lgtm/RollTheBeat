RollTheBeat : Comment jouer ?

1: Introduction

Bienvenue dans RollTheBeat, un jeu basé sur ta capacité à deviner un morceau aussi vite qu'un vrai fan Hardcore, ou à l'interpréter tel un véritable Guitar Hero.

2: Mise en pla.

3: Comment jouer

On your turn, roll the dice and match the numbers to the beat of the playing music. Higher matches score points.

4: Score

Points are awarded based on accuracy and speed. The player with the most points at the end wins.

5: Fin du jeu

The game ends after a set number of rounds. The player with the highest score is declared the winner.
